Overview

This is a stop motion animation using Lego toys and a Bumblebee model, collaborating with Lei Hong and Ying Yang. The story is set in a time when Bumblebee is in dead sleep, and a group of people want to awake him and use the force of Bumblebee to destory the city where spider man lives in.

Role in this project: storyboard, editing, visual effects.
