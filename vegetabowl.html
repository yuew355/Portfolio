Overview

For this project, I designed an app for people who want to keep fit which can recommend custom-built salad and decide and record how much nutrition they should intake according to users’ body data.

Research

With the development of the economy, more and more people around the world start to pay attention to body building or keep a diet. It is reported that one out of 8 dollars is used for body building in US. In China, over half of people are used to doing exercise to keep fit. Because of its low calories and high nutrition, salad becomes popular in these people. According to a survey, over 63% people who want to keep fit would have salad every day. Salad’s various kinds provides many choices for these people, and bring trouble on choosing which kind is better for their body at the same time.

Comparison

There exists some apps that help people with their diet. One of them is to add food and its weight to calculate calories and other nutrients. However, sometimes it can be a trouble as users can not reckon the weight or how much flavoring they add.

The other kind of apps provide salad menu and ingredients for users, though they pay no attention to their nutrients and calories, which is inconvenient for users who want to keep fit.

It is hard to find a product which could order or provide salad menu and calculate all kinds of nutrients to provide a better diet at the same time.
